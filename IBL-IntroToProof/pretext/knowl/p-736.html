There exist operations \(+\) (addition) and \(\cdot\) (multiplication) on \(\mathbb{R}\) satisfying:
  1. (Associativity for Addition) For all \(a,b, c\in \mathbb{R}\) we have \((a+b)+c = a+(b+c)\text{;}\)
  2. (Commutativity for Addition) For all \(a,b\in \mathbb{R}\text{,}\) we have \(a+b=b+a\text{;}\)
  3. (Additive Identity) There exists \(0\in\mathbb{R}\) such that for all \(a\in\mathbb{R}\text{,}\) \(0+a=a\text{;}\)
  4. (Additive Inverses) For all \(a\in\mathbb{R}\) there exists \(-a\in\mathbb{R}\) such that \(a+(-a)=0\text{;}\)
  5. (Associativity for Multiplication) For all \(a,b, c\in \mathbb{R}\) we have \((ab)c = a(bc)\text{;}\)
  6. (Commutativity for Multiplication) For all \(a,b\in \mathbb{R}\text{,}\) we have \(ab=ba\text{;}\)
  7. (Multiplicative Identity) There exists \(1\in\mathbb{R}\) such that \(1\neq 0\) and for all \(a\in\mathbb{R}\text{,}\) \(1a=a\text{;}\)
  8. (Multiplicative Inverses) For all \(a\in\mathbb{R}\setminus\{0\}\) there exists \(a^{-1}\in\mathbb{R}\) such that \(aa^{-1}=1\text{.}\)
  9. (Distributive Property) For all \(a,b,c\in \mathbb{R}\text{,}\) \(a(b+c)=ab+ac\text{;}\)
