Definition 8.87.

Let \(f:X\to Y\) be a function.
  1. If \(S\subseteq X\text{,}\) the image of \(S\) under \(f\) is defined via
    \begin{equation*} \tcboxmath{f(S)\coloneqq \{f(x) \mid x\in S\}}\text{.} \end{equation*}
  2. If \(T\subseteq Y\text{,}\) the preimage (or inverse image) of \(T\) under \(f\) is defined via
    \begin{equation*} \tcboxmath{f^{-1}(T)\coloneqq \{x\in X \mid f(x)\in T\}}\text{.} \end{equation*}
