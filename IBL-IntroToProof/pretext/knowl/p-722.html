It turns out that the Well-Ordering Principle (Theorem 4.39) and the Axiom of Induction (Axiom 4.1) are equivalent. In other words, one can prove the Well-Ordering Principle from the Axiom of Induction, as we have done, but one can also prove the Axiom of Induction if the Well-Ordering Principle is assumed.
