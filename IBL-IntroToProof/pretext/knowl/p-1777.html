Do not interchange \({=}\) and \({\implies}\text{.}\) The equals sign connects two objects, as in “\(x^2=b\)”; the symbol “\(\implies\)” is an abbreviation for “implies” and connects two statements, as in “\(a+b=a \implies b=0\text{.}\)” You should avoid using \(\implies\) in formal write-ups of proofs.
