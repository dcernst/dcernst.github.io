Problem 8.44.

  1. What conditions on \(R\) must hold in order for \(f:A\to \Rel(R)\) defined via \(f(a)=\rel(a)\) to be a function?
  2. What additional conditions, if any, must hold on \(R\) in order for \(f\) to be a surjective function?
