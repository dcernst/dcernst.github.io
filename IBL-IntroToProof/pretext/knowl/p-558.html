Let \(\{A_{\alpha}\}_{\alpha\in\Delta}\) be a collection of sets. Then
  1. \(\displaystyle \left(\bigcup_{\alpha\in\Delta} A_{\alpha}\right)^C=\bigcap_{\alpha\in\Delta}A_{\alpha}^{C}\text{,}\) and
  2. \(\displaystyle \left(\bigcap_{\alpha\in\Delta} A_{\alpha}\right)^C=\bigcup_{\alpha\in\Delta}A_{\alpha}^{C}\text{.}\)
