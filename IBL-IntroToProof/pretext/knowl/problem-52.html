Problem 3.23.

For each of the statements (a)–(d) on the left, find an equivalent symbolic proposition chosen from the list (i)–(v) on the right. Note that not every statement on the right will get used.
  1. \(A \nsubseteq B\text{.}\)
  2. \(A \cap B= \emptyset\text{.}\)
  3. \((A \cup B)^{c} \neq \emptyset\text{.}\)
  4. \((A \cap B)^{c} = \emptyset\text{.}\)
  1. \(\displaystyle (\forall x)(x \in A \wedge x \in B)\)
  2. \(\displaystyle (\forall x)(x \in A \implies x \notin B)\)
  3. \(\displaystyle (\exists x)(x \notin A \wedge x \notin B)\)
  4. \(\displaystyle (\exists x)(x \in A \vee x \in B)\)
  5. \(\displaystyle (\exists x)(x \in A \wedge x\notin B)\)
