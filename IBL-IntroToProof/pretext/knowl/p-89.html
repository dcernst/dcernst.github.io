Given \(n,m\in\mathbb{Z}\text{,}\) we say that \(n\) divides \(m\text{,}\) written \(\tcboxmath{n|m}\text{,}\) if there exists \(k\in\mathbb{Z}\) such that \(m=nk\text{.}\) If \(n|m\text{,}\) we may also say that \(m\) is divisible by \(n\) or that \(n\) is a factor of \(m\text{.}\)
