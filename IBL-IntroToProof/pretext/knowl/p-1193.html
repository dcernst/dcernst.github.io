Recall that Theorem 7.59 says that the equivalence classes for a relation on a nonempty set \(A\) determines a partition of \(A\text{.}\) The following theorem tells us that every partition of a set yields an equivalence relation where the equivalence classes correspond to the blocks of the partition. This result is a consequence of Theorems 7.68, Theorem 7.70, and Theorem 7.71.
