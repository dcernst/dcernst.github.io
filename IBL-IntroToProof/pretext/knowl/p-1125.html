Let \(A=\{a,b,c,d,e\}\text{.}\)
  1. Make up an equivalence relation \(\sim\) on \(A\) by drawing a digraph such that \(a\) is not related to \(b\) and \(c\) is not related to \(b\text{.}\)
  2. Using your digraph, find \(\Rel(\sim)\) by determining \(\rel(x)\) for each \(x\in A\text{.}\)
