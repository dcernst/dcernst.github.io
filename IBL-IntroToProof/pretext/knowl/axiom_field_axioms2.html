Axiom 5.1.1 (Field Axioms).

There exist operations \(+\) (addition) and \(\cdot\) (multiplication) on \(\mathbb{R}\) satisfying:
  1. (F1) (Associativity for Addition) For all \(a,b, c\in \mathbb{R}\) we have \((a+b)+c = a+(b+c)\text{;}\)
  2. (F2) (Commutativity for Addition) For all \(a,b\in \mathbb{R}\text{,}\) we have \(a+b=b+a\text{;}\)
  3. (F3) (Additive Identity) There exists \(0\in\mathbb{R}\) such that for all \(a\in\mathbb{R}\text{,}\) \(0+a=a\text{;}\)
  4. (F4) (Additive Inverses) For all \(a\in\mathbb{R}\) there exists \(-a\in\mathbb{R}\) such that \(a+(-a)=0\text{;}\)
