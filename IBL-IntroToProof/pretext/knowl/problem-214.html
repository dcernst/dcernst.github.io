  3. \(\displaystyle f((-2,4))\)

  5. \(\displaystyle f^{-1}((-3,3))\)
  6. \(\displaystyle f(\emptyset)\)
  7. \(\displaystyle f(\mathbb{R})\)
  8. \(\displaystyle f^{-1}(\{-1\})\)
  9. \(\displaystyle f^{-1}(\emptyset)\)
  10. \(\displaystyle f^{-1}(\mathbb{R})\)
