Problem 5.69.

Consider the open interval \(I=(1,2)\text{.}\) Prove each of the following.
  1. The points \(1\) and \(2\) are accumulation points of \(I\text{.}\)
  2. If \(p\in I\text{,}\) then \(p\) is an accumulation point of \(I\text{.}\)
  3. If \(p\lt 1\) or \(p>2\text{,}\) then \(p\) is not an accumulation point of \(I\text{.}\)
