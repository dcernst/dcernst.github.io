The upshot of Parts (i) and (l) of Problems 5.60 and Problem 5.75 is that \(\mathbb{R}\) and \(\emptyset\) are both open and closed. It turns out that these are the only two subsets of the real numbers with this property. One issue with the terminology that could potentially create confusion is that the open interval \((-\infty, \infty)\) is both an open and a closed set.
