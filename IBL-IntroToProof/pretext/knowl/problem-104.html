Problem 5.83.

Determine whether each of the following sets is open, closed, both, or neither.
  1. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle V=\bigcup_{n=2}^{\infty} \left(n - \frac{1}{2},n\right)\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle W=\bigcap_{n=2}^{\infty} \left(n - \frac{1}{2},n\right)\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle X=\bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty} \left(-\frac{1}{n}, \frac{1}{n}\right)\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle Y=\bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty} \left(-n, n\right)\)
  5. \(\displaystyle Z=(0,1)\cap \mathbb{Q}\)
