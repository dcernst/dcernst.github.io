Problem 9.35.

Quickly verify that the following sets are infinite by appealing to either Theorem 9.31 (use (ii) implies (i)) or Corollary 9.32.
  1. Set of odd natural numbers
  2. Set of even natural numbers
  3. \(\displaystyle \mathbb{Z}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \mathbb{N}\times \mathbb{N}\)
  5. \(\displaystyle \mathbb{Q}\)
  6. \(\displaystyle \mathbb{R}\)
  7. Set of perfect squares in \(\mathbb{N}\)
  8. \(\displaystyle (0,1)\)
  9. \(\displaystyle \mathbb{C}\coloneqq \{a+bi\mid a,b\in\mathbb{R}\}\)
