For \(a,b\in\mathbb{R}\) with \(a\lt b\text{,}\) we define the following sets, referred to as intervals.
  1. \(\displaystyle \tcboxmath{(a,b)\coloneqq \{x\in\mathbb{R}\mid a\lt x\lt b\}}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \tcboxmath{[a,b]\coloneqq \{x\in\mathbb{R}\mid a\leq x\leq b\}}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \tcboxmath{[a,b)\coloneqq \{x\in\mathbb{R}\mid a\leq x\lt b\}}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \tcboxmath{(a,\infty)\coloneqq \{x\in\mathbb{R}\mid a\lt x\}}\)
  5. \(\displaystyle \tcboxmath{(-\infty,b)\coloneqq \{x\in\mathbb{R}\mid x\lt b\}}\)
  6. \(\displaystyle \tcboxmath{(-\infty,\infty)\coloneqq \mathbb{R}}\)
