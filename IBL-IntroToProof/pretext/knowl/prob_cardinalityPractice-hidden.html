  1. \(\displaystyle \card(\{a,b,c\})=\card(\{x,y,z\})\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \card(\mathbb{N})=\card(\{2n+1\mid n\in \mathbb{N}\})\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \card(\mathbb{N})=\card(\mathbb{Z})\)
  4. \(\card((a,b))=\card((c,d))\) (where \((a,b)\) and \((c,d)\) are intervals)
  5. \(\displaystyle \card(\mathbb{N})=\card\left(\left\{\frac{1}{2^n}\mid n\in \mathbb{N}\right\}\right)\)
