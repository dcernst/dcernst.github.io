Let \(A\) and \(B\) be sets in some universe of discourse \(U\text{.}\)
  1. The union of the sets \(A\) and \(B\) is \(\tcboxmath{A \cup B \coloneqq \{x\in U \mid x\in A \mbox{ or } x\in B \}}\text{.}\)
  2. The intersection of the sets \(A\) and \(B\) is \(\tcboxmath{A \cap B \coloneqq\{x\in U \mid x\in A \mbox{ and } x\in B \}}\text{.}\)
  3. The set difference of the sets \(A\) and \(B\) is \(\tcboxmath{A \setminus B \coloneqq\{x\in U \mid x\in A \mbox{ and } x\notin B \}}\text{.}\)
  4. The complement of \(A\) (relative to \(U\)) is the set \(\tcboxmath{A^c\coloneqq U \setminus A =\{x \in U \mid x \notin A\}}\text{.}\)
