Say exactly what you mean. Just as \(=\) is sometimes abused, so too people sometimes write \(A\in B\) when they mean \(A\subseteq B\text{,}\) or write \(a_{ij}\in A\) when they mean that \(a_{ij}\) is an entry in matrix \(A\text{.}\) Mathematics is a very precise language, and there is a way to say exactly what you mean; find it and use it.
