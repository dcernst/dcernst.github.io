Suppose that the universe of discourse is \(U=\mathbb{R}\text{.}\) Let \(A=[-3,-1)\text{,}\) \(B=(-2.5,2)\text{,}\) and \(C=(-2,0]\text{.}\) Find each of the following.
  1. \(\displaystyle A^c\)
  2. \(\displaystyle A \cap C\)
  3. \(\displaystyle A \cap B\)
  4. \(\displaystyle A \cup B\)
  5. \(\displaystyle (A\cap B)^c\)
  6. \(\displaystyle (A\cup B)^c\)
  7. \(\displaystyle A \setminus B\)
  8. \(\displaystyle A\setminus (B \cup C)\)
  9. \(\displaystyle B \setminus A\)
