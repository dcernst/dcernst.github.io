The Infinite Hotel has rooms numbered \(1,2,3,4,\ldots\text{.}\) Every room in the Infinite Hotel is currently occupied.
  1. Is it possible to make room for one more guest (assuming they want a room all to themselves)?
  2. An infinite number of new guests, say \(g_1, g_2,g_3,\ldots\text{,}\) show up in the lobby and each demands a room. Is it possible to make room for all the new guests even if the hotel is already full?
