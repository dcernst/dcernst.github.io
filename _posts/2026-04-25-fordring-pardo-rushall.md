This group of undergraduate research students (Shaye Fordring, Seb Pardo, and Elenor Rushall, together with Casey Malone) has been a blast to work with! We’ve been studying pattern avoidance in signed Cayley permutations using combinatorial species (blah, blah, blah...fancy-sounding mathematics).  The pics are from this year's [Southwestern Undergraduate Mathematics Research Conference](https://naumathstat.github.io/sunmarc2026/) (SUnMaRC), which NAU hosted this year, and the [NAU Undergraduate Symposium](https://in.nau.edu/office-undergraduate-research/undergraduate-symposium/).
